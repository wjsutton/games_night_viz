# Level 1: Player Select Roundup
8th Nov - 31st Dec 2021

Our first #GamesNightViz project had the theme "Player Select", which follows the start of most games - choosing your character. 

Throughout the end of 2021 we received 25 submissions! With visualisations covering a several games but some popular choices included: 
- Pokemon, 
- Street Fighter V 
- and HowChoo.com's top video game icons
